About Us

Founded in 1984 by Mr. Ajmal Khan Mohammad, AKM Music Centre has been at the heart of the UAE’s music scene for over four decades.We are dedicated to bringing the joy of music to everyone — from passionate beginners to seasoned professionals. Our commitment is simple: to offer the finest instruments, expert guidance, and services that inspire creativity.

Whether you need an instrument, a repair, a rental, a music class, or a professional studio, AKM Music is here to make your musical journey seamless and unforgettable.

Accepted payments: Cash, COD, Debit & Credit Card, Tabby, Cheque, Installments, PayPal.

Visit us at our showroom in Abu Dhabi or Select one of our services below and start your next musical chapter.
