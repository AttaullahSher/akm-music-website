About AKM Music

Leading music store in Abu Dhabi since 1984, dedicated to excellence in instruments, education, and services

Our Story

Founded in 1984 by Ajmal Khan Mohammed, AKM Music has been a cornerstone of Abu Dhabi's music community for nearly four decades. What started as a small music shop has evolved into a comprehensive music center offering a wide range of services.

Our mission is to inspire musicians of all levels by providing premium instruments, professional services, and quality music education. We believe that music has the power to transform lives, and we're committed to making it accessible to everyone.

Today, AKM Music stands as one of the most trusted names in the Gulf for musical instruments, repairs, rentals, and music classes.
